Hello my name is Vonoa Fiddlecord

I was the only Apothacary in my hometown, studying medical plants and medicine for my entire life, under my mentor Zilmop. Then a strange, seemingly magical disease swept through the town while I was gone on a journey to the next town. I came back and nearly everyone was dying. Zilmop was the third to die , I tried to save everyone else but i couldn't do more than ease their pain. Now I'm on the road seaching for answers.

I was able to meet a group of people and together we took a job from a man Named Hosea to escort his niece Vera, but on our journey we became trapped in a strange land called Barovia ruled by a vampire named Strahd.
